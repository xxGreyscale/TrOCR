Noto Sans Tangsa is a design for the Indic Tangsa script.

Noto Sans Tangsa has multiple weights, contains 94 glyphs, 2 OpenType features, and supports 93 characters from the Unicode block Tangsa.

Supported writing systems

Tangsa

Tangsa is an Indic alphabet. Used by the Tangsa (Tangshang, Hawa) people at the border between India and Myanmar. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, r12a.
